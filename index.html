About Me

Professional Profile

Your Partner in Mental Wellness

I'm Sehar Faiz, a Clinical Psychologist with over 5 years of experience providing online and in-person psychological services to children, adolescents, and adults.

Education

I hold a BS in Applied Psychology from the University of the Punjab and an MS in Clinical Psychology from Lahore Garrison University.

Experience

My clinical experience spans hospitals, clinics, and educational settings, where I have provided psychological assessment and therapy to children, adolescents, and adults experiencing diverse emotional, behavioral, developmental, and mental health challenges.

Mission

My mission is to provide accessible, compassionate mental health care that empowers every person to heal, grow, and live authentically. I believe everyone deserves a safe space to be heard and supported without judgment.

Vision

I envision a world where seeking therapy is normalized and celebrated — where mental health is prioritized with the same importance as physical health, and where every individual has the tools to thrive emotionally.
