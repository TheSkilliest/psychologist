About Sehar Faiz

Sehar Faiz is a Clinical Psychologist based in Lahore, Punjab, Pakistan, with academic training in Clinical Psychology and Applied Psychology. She has worked across clinical, educational, and special-needs support settings, providing evidence-based care through assessment, psychotherapy, psychoeducation, and collaborative treatment planning.

Her experience includes working with individuals, couples, and families while addressing concerns such as anxiety, depression, trauma, relationship difficulties, and emotional distress. Her professional approach emphasizes compassion, confidentiality, structured assessment, and tailored therapeutic support.

Therapeutic Approach

Sehar Faiz follows a client-centered and evidence-based approach to therapy. Her work emphasizes careful assessment, individualized treatment planning, emotional safety, and collaborative care that responds to the unique needs of each client.


She also values multidisciplinary coordination where needed, ensuring that support is thoughtful, responsible, and aligned with best practices in mental health care.
